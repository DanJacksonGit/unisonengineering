ABOUT

Established in 2006 by Paul Sheehan, multi Enterprise Award winning Unison Engineering Services are one of Ireland's leading suppliers and producers of equipment for the food, beverage and liquid processing industries.

Unison specialise in Pasteurisers, Homogenisers, Heat Exchangers, Pumps and Valves along with 24/7 on-call Servicing and Testing.

Clients range from multinational corporations to artisan producers. Unison Engineering's Limerick facility is the hub of business, housing a fully fledged design and manufacturing centre along with a dedicated 24/7 service and testing team. This team of fully qualified engineers have combined experience of more than 80 years in the processing industries. As a result of this, Unison understand the importance of combining speed with expert advice at competitive prices.
